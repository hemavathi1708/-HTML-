Hidden Love

Hidden Love is a romantic C-drama that focuses on a sweet and slow-burn love story. It shows how friendship gradually turns into love over time.
The drama is popular for its gentle emotions, realistic characters, and heartwarming moments. It is especially loved by young audiences.

Love Between Fairy and Devil

This drama is a fantasy romance that combines magic, destiny, and love. It tells the story of a fairy and a powerful demon lord whose lives become connected by fate.
The drama is known for its beautiful visuals, emotional storyline, and strong character development.

The Untamed

The Untamed is a fantasy historical drama based on friendship, loyalty, and moral values. It follows two cultivators who uncover dark
secrets while standing up for justice. The drama is praised for its deep story, strong bond between characters, and traditional Chinese elements.

Ashes of Love
