RM

KIM NAMJOON

RM is the leader of BTS and plays a vital role in guiding the group. He is known for his intelligence, calm nature, and thoughtful leadership. RM has a deep love for literature, philosophy, and art,
which strongly influences his songwriting. Many of BTS’s songs contain deep meanings because of his lyrical contribution.
He learned English by watching TV shows and interviews, which later helped BTS communicate with international audiences.
RM often speaks on behalf of the group in global events and delivers speeches that inspire young people. He encourages self-reflection, growth, and acceptance. As a leader, he respects each member’s individuality and brings the group together with understanding and maturity.

JIN

KIM SEOKJIN

Jin is the oldest member of BTS and is known for his warm personality and sense of humor. He is often described as someone who creates a comfortable and positive atmosphere within the group.
Jin is also a strong vocalist with a clear and emotional voice that adds depth to BTS’s music.
Despite joining the group without much prior experience in singing or dancing, Jin worked extremely hard to improve his skills. His dedication shows the importance of perseverance and self-belief.
Jin is also known for hiscaring nature and often takes responsibility for looking after the members. His confidence and optimism inspire fans to accept themselves as they are.

SUGA

MIN YOONGI

Suga is a rapper, songwriter, and music producer who is admired for his honesty and passion for music. His lyrics often talk about real-life struggles,
such as pressure, fear, ambition, and self-doubt. Through his music, Suga encourages listeners to face reality and not feel alone in their challenges.
He has a quiet personality but a strong presence in music production. Suga has been involved in composing and producing many BTS songs.
His journey shows how creativity can be a powerful way to express emotions. He believes in hard work and staying true to oneself, which makes him relatable to many fans around the world.
